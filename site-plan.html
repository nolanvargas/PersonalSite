Purpose

This site is designed by Thomas Vargas for the purpose of displaying his portfolio. This will contain his information, history of job experience, as well as projects that he will be working on over the course of his degree.

The website will be easily accessible to potential future employers to look over when it comes to searching for a job in the future as well as anyone interested in learning more about him.
